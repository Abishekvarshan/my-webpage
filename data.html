Data Deletion Request

If you would like to delete your account data associated with the n8n App Integration, please follow the instructions below:

Steps

  1. Send an email to varsityabi@gmail.com with the subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  2. Include your name, email address, and Meta account ID (if applicable).
  3. Your request will be processed, and all associated data will be deleted within 7 business days.

For urgent requests, please contact us directly at varsityabi@gmail.com.
